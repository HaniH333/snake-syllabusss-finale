Can't Tell if It's Venomous?

North America has many harmless snakes that mimic venomous ones. When in doubt — back away slowly. Never handle a wild snake unless trained to do so.

🔴
Red touches yellow Coral Snake — neurotoxic venom, seek emergency care
VENOMOUS
⚫
Red touches black Milksnake or Scarlet Kingsnake — completely harmless
HARMLESS
📐
Triangular head + heat pits Likely a pit viper — Rattlesnake, Copperhead or Cottonmouth
CAUTION
🔔
Rattle on tail Rattlesnake — back away, do not provoke or handle
VENOMOUS
